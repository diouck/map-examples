Geoadmin and poor man's time serie

history, dufour, siegfried, time serie, geoadmin,

Uses GeoAdmin to several map side by side.

Maps from different period are snapshot of the landscape. Displaying thesemaps side-by-side enables historians and enthousiaste alike to see the evolution of the landscape and mapping techniques.

The three displayed maps are (left to right):

  • Carte Dufour (1845 to 1865)
  • Carte Siegfried (1870 to 1926)
  • Carte national (today)

View the time-serie.js source to see how this is done.
