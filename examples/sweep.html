Sweeping a layer on another one with GeoAdmin API.

Sweeping a layer on another offer the possibility to quickly compare two maps. Click on the orange bar and move it right on left.

Left is the Siefried Map from late 19th century and right the Dufour Map from mid 19th century, centered on the city of Geneva.

You will notice the destruction of the fortification, new boulevards and the train station.
