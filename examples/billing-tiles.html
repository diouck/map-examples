Billing Tiles GeoAdmin Example

billing, money, not cheap, api, swisstopo, geoadmin, openlayers

Display your tiles consumption in real time

Show you your tiles consumption, based on OpenLayers.Tile.Image.onImageLoad. It may actually differs from the actual tiles consumption charged by swisstopo, based on server logs, as nor JavaScriptOpenLayers nor OpenLayers have ways to know if a tile comes from the browser cache or was requested on the server.

View the billing-tiles.js source to see how this is done.
