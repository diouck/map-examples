Sometime, layers which logically belongs together are in two separated layers. In this example we'll combine the 'CadastralWebMap (ch.kantone.cadastralwebmap-farbe)at large scale, with the the TLM-Map (ch.swisstopo.tml3d-hintergrund-karte) at smaller one. This is done using a so called aggregate layer we'll give the name org.procrastinatio.combined-background and by defining new sclae limits.

Please, remember, both canton Wallis and Sankt-Gallen have not published their cadastral map, and some area in the mountains are also not covered ba this map.

View the combined-layers.js source to see how this is done.
