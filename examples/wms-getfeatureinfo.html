Highlighting the clicked feature with WMS GetFeatureInfo.

One of the major drawback of the WMS GetFeatureInfo request is that you can't easily highlight the clicked feature. Well that what I though. Actually the return format of a WMS GetFeatureInfo is poorly defined in the standard and you may return a full GML document. Then it is easy to parse this GML and to add it to your map.


For Mapserver, check the doc, and especially the gml_geometries and gml_[geometry name]_type parameters.


View the wms-getfeatureinfo.js source to see how this is done.
