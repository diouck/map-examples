Mashup with Ski- and snowshoes tour infos (password-protected).

If you ski- or snowshoes-touring, you may find a good deal of useful informations online. Unfortunately, it's hard to combine the ski- and snowshoes-routes you have in Swiss Map online, with the wildlife sanctury of Respektiere deine Grenze, with the ability to load KMLs of map.geo.admin.ch.So this very basic webpage is for you.

Only the newest "blue" leisure maps from swisstopo have showshoes route inprints. Check for them on swisstopo.

Disclaimer: this is only an example pagesome informations shown on the map do not fit with the observations on the field. On tour, you are on your own!

On a technical side, three layers (ski- and snowshoes-route and slopes) are configured with a custom config files, while the s for the two wildlife sanctuary layers are retrieved from a WMTS GetCapabilities document, which may take time.

View the ski.js source to see how this is done (ugly!).
